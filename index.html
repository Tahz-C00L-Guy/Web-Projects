Our Heritage

A Family Legacy
of Ironcraft

Founded in 1990 in the UAE, Star Wrought Iron has spent over three decades mastering the ancient art of wrought iron fabrication. What began as a family workshop has grown into the Northern Emirates' most trusted name in bespoke metalwork — serving luxury residential estates, landmark commercial developments, and heritage restoration projects across the region.

"Every piece carries the weight of tradition and the precision of modern craftsmanship — hand-shaped, hand-finished, built to endure."

Our artisans — many of whom have worked with the family for over two decades — bring an intimate knowledge of iron's character to every commission. From grand estate gates to delicate interior screens, the hand is always present.
